It's not linear — it loops

Draw the arrows and the "process" collapses into a few tight feedback loops. These are where people get stuck, re-decide, and abandon.

Fresh copy ↔ Other documents ↔ 3-month expiry
Every bigger file (visa, passport, job) takes longer than 3 months to assemble — so the ikhraj qaid expires mid-file and you start over. The most common loop in Lebanese paperwork.
Which version? ↔ Which institution?
Fardi vs 3a2ili vs Nufus extract — each office names a different one, often after you've already paid for the wrong one.
Mukhtar's hours ↔ My work hours
He works when I work. Catching him is days of phone tag or a half-day gamble — multiplied by every fresh copy.
