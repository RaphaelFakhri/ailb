It's not linear — it loops

Draw the arrows and the "process" collapses into a few tight feedback loops. These are where buyers get stuck, re-decide, and abandon — or buy scared and overpay at a shop.

Good price ↔ Scam fear
The better the deal, the less I trust it. With no verification layer, price stops carrying information — so buyers distrust the whole market equally.
Vetting questions ↔ Seller ghosts
Ask for IMEI and a video call and half the sellers vanish — including honest ones who just can't be bothered. Back to the hunt.
Checklist ↔ Meetup pressure
Everyone knows what to check at home; almost nobody checks it all with the seller standing there. The knowledge exists — it fails at the exact moment it matters.
