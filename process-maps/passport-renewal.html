It's not linear — it loops

Draw the arrows and the "process" collapses into a few tight feedback loops. These are where people get stuck, re-decide, and abandon.

Appointment date ↔ Ikhraj qaid freshness
The slot you finally get is far enough away that the ikhraj qaid you got for it expires first. The two clocks are never introduced to each other.
Demand spikes ↔ Platform collapse ↔ Brokers
Emigration waves and summer travel hit at once; slots evaporate; a paid layer of fixers appears; trust in the platform drops, so people over-book. 2022 in one sentence.
"Valid" passport ↔ 6-month airline rule
A passport with 5 months left is legally valid and practically dead. People discover the loop at the check-in counter.
