It's not linear — it loops

Draw the arrows and the "process" collapses into a few tight feedback loops. These are where people get stuck, re-decide, and abandon.

Slot date ↔ Document freshness
Book a slot two months out and your bank statement and ikhraj qaid age out before you reach the counter. You re-issue, and sometimes re-book.
Reservations ↔ Visa
Can't buy real tickets without the visa; can't get the visa without showing tickets. The whole fake-booking economy exists inside this loop.
Refusal ↔ Reapplication
Each refusal stamps the record and weakens the next file — so people over-document, which delays the file, which expires the documents.
