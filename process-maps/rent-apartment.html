It's not linear — it loops

Draw the arrows and the "process" collapses into a few tight feedback loops. These are where renters get stuck, re-decide, and abandon.

Rent ↔ Moteur math ↔ Neighborhood
A cheap flat with a $120 moteur bill costs the same as a pricier one with better power. Every "affordable" find reopens the neighborhood question.
Listing ↔ Visit ↔ Disappointment
Photos lie, so you visit; the visit disappoints, so you scroll again. Each cycle burns an evening and a taxi fare, and the search restarts from zero.
Avoid the simsar ↔ Fewer listings
Dodge the one-month fee and half the market disappears; crawl back to the simsar and the budget breaks. Most people loop here until urgency decides for them.
